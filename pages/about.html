About Lumen Media

We turn videos into content, and content into clients.

At Lumen Media, our journey began with a passion for storytelling and a drive to help brands connect through compelling content. Today, we deliver end-to-end social media management including video editing, content posting, and engagement handling.

Our Mission

To elevate brands by producing scroll-stopping content, strategically posted and actively managed to generate real engagement and results.

About Me – Amritpal Singh

My journey began when I was just 15 years old, glued to my computer, exploring everything digital. Over time, I discovered my passion—video editing—and started creating my own content, uploading it to YouTube, and learning the ropes of social media. But life had other plans, and I had to step away for a while.

The fire never died, though. In November 2020, at 19, I took my first professional step as a short-form video editor and thumbnail designer for a small company. That’s where the real grind began. Fast forward to today—I’ve worked with multiple clients on monthly retainers, all of whom came to me organically because they believed in what I could do for their brands.

Now, I’m expanding Lumen Media to help businesses and creators actually grow—not just post blindly, but build an audience that engages, trusts, and buys.

Why We Exist

I didn’t choose this field because it’s a "make money from home" hustle—there are easier ways to do that. I’m here because I truly believe everyone deserves the right to grow.

When I started YouTube, I had no guidance—no mentor to point me in the right direction. I wasted years figuring things out alone. If I’d had someone to help me back then, I’d be three years ahead of where I am today.

That’s why Lumen Media exists. So you don’t have to lose those years.

Social media shouldn’t be a guessing game. You shouldn’t have to grind in the dark, hoping something sticks. Whether you’re a brand wanting more customers or a creator dreaming of a loyal fanbase—we’re here to give you the strategy, the polish, and the consistency you need to make it happen.

You’ve got the talent. We’ve got the blueprint. Let’s grow—together.
